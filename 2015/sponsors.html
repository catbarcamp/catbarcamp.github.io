Becoming a Sponsor

Becoming a CAT Barcamp sponsor is a great way to support an awesome event. To ask questions about sponsoring catbarcamp@cat.pdx.edu.

What your money will be spent on

CAT BarCamp is entirely run by volunteers; no one is taking a salary for its production. Funds will be spent to provide a morning snack, refreshments throughout the day, lunch at noon for attendees, and a tee shirt for all participants. Any funds remaining will be rolled over into the funding for CAT BarCamp 2, next September.

Who will be there

TheCAT at PSU is an ops training organization that is happy to help secure the venue and organize the event. We expect many job-hungry students skilled in development, computer science, operations, and everything else to be in attendance at this event and we want to enable as many connections as possible.

Benefits of Becoming a Sponsor

Most of the attendees of this conference are not purchasers or decision makers, but they are the next generation of engineers. This is a great opportunity for your recruitment team to meet the students and for the students to meet your companies. For sponsors, we can provide a table in the atrium where everyone will gather in the morning, at lunch, and at breaks. We will feature your name/logo prominently it all posters/media/t-shirt, and we will recognize you formally at the morning opening speech. For sponsors at the "Gold" level or higher, we can dedicate a 50 minute session to you for whatever you want to do, including a "meet the engineers" session or a product demo.

Bronze $100 donation to CAT Barcamp

  • Name/logo on all promotional materials & tee shirt (shirts ordered for CAT Barcamp 2014, new sponsors before that date will get their logo on other promotional materials only.)
  • Recognition by the opening speaker

Silver $250 donation to CAT Barcamp

  • Name/logo on all promotional materials & tee shirt (shirts ordered for CAT Barcamp 2014, new sponsors before that date will get their logo on other promotional materials only.)
  • Recognition by the opening speaker
  • A table in the atrium for your firm

Gold $500 donation to CAT Barcamp

  • Name/logo on all promotional materials & tee shirt (shirts ordered for CAT Barcamp 2014, new sponsors before that date will get their logo on other promotional materials only.)
  • Recognition by the opening speaker
  • A table in the atrium for your firm
  • If you wish, we will also block out one 50 minute session for you to interact with attendees in a designated room. This could be a "meet the engineers" session, a product demo, or a game of settlers of catan.
