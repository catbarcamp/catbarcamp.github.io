What a Session looks like

There will be an option to give a 25 min session or a 50 min session. The session topics can cover a variety of topics. Special equipment is not necessary to give a talk but if you'd like media, please let our schedulers know as not all of our rooms will be equipped with the same things.

The process for signing up is simple; you will be given a sticky note (a whole one for a full session and a half one for half a session). Write the topic you will be speaking about and your name. Then place the sticky note in the timeslot you would like to speak in.

Attendees will be given the opportunity to vote for the sessions they plan on going to. This will give the schedulers a good idea of what room size to place you in.
