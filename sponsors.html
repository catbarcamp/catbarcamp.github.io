Becoming a Sponsor

Becoming a CAT Barcamp sponsor is a great way to support an awesome event. To become a sponsor contact us at catbarcamp@cat.pdx.edu.

Benifits of Becoming a Sponsor

Bronze $100 donation to CAT Barcamp

  • Name/logo on all promotional materials & tee shirt
  • Recognition by the opening speaker

Silver $250 donation to CAT Barcamp

  • Name/logo on all promotional materials & tee shirt
  • Recognition by the opening speaker
  • A table in the atrium for your firm

Gold $500 donation to CAT Barcamp

  • Name/logo on all promotional materials & tee shirt
  • Recognition by the opening speaker
  • A table in the atrium for your firm
  • If you wish, we will also block out one 50 minute session for you to interact with attendees in a designated room. This could be a "meet the engineers" session, a product demo, or a game of settlers of catan.
